About us

ImpleMentors

Nurturing Minds, Inspiring Excellence, Expanding Horizons. Led by acclaimed UN Awardee and G20 speaker, Mr. Rahul Menon, our freelance academic initiative revolutionizes the education industry by empowering students with comprehensive training in diverse disciplines. From honing public speaking and communication skills to fostering personality development and intellectual growth, we equip students with the tools to succeed. Our team specializes in areas such as International Relations, Law, Economics, Policy-Making, and more. Through engaging in dynamic platforms like MUNs and Youth Parliaments, we provide students with practical experience and cultivate their critical thinking abilities. With our guidance, students are prepared to excel in various competitive exams like UPSC, CDS, and IPR. Join us to unlock your true potential, broaden your career opportunities, and embark on a transformative educational journey.

About the Chief Advisor

Mr.Rauhl Menon, The Chief Trainer and Advisor

Mr. Rahul Menon is a well-established public speaker and an international relations specialist. He has been working with multiple institutions across the globe for mentoring students. He has been appreciated for his enthusiastic support to his students in the Times of India and Hindustan Times among other leading media agencies. He is the only person in India to have been awarded by the United Nations for being the Best Mentor across 91,000 institutions. He was also recently invited as a speaker by the G20 under the aegis of W20 to interact with the students from all walks of life at Delhi Technological University. Further, His contribution towards the field has empowered multiple aspirants in achieving their dreams and pursuing a life with dignity.
